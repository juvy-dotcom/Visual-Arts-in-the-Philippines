Interviews and Opinions

Patrick Flores

Opinion:" I learned art history within a Humanities perspective, a liberal arts orientation that was more integrative than specialist. In many ways, it was interdisciplinary, inflected by cultural politics that may have come from activism and anthropology."

Source: https://mulatmapatusapan.up.edu.ph/an-interview-with-dr-patrick-flores

Allice Guillermo

Opinion: She explained that " Aesthetics is not just about the appreciation of artistic value to an art or to how great it look, aesthetics is the philosophy of art that focuses on the understanding of the idea behind the art and its nature, in line with the factors affecting its expressiveness which is mostly from the certain condition of time and place. "

Source: https://artsthoughtsandphilosophy.wordpress.com/2018/12/01/review-on-alice-guillermos-contemporary-philippine-aesthetics/

Cid Reyes

Opinion:"This profession is not for the faint of heart, this is especially true during the pandemic when artists, they be in visual or performance arts have been among the most affected, professionally and economically". He countered that by saying " Painting is important because it allows us to communicate ideas, elevates us beyond brutishness, and allows for individual style or expression."

Source: https://www.thediarist.ph/cid-reyes-what-he-does-is-not-for-the-faint-of-heart/

Buen Calubayan

Opinion:"In my point of view the paintings recognize the beauty and simplicity of life of the Filipinos. It creates a wonderful painting that symbolizes the simple and meaningful life of a Filipino that despite of the storms we’ve encounter, we stand as a one Filipino."

Source: https://www.manilatimes.net

Neil Pasilan

Opinion:“My multi-layered paintings invite viewers to explore the intricacies of the human experience and the beauty of imperfections.”

Source: https://pinaywise.com/philippines-facts/discover-philippine-contemporary-art-now/?srsltid=AfmBOoolG8gHD7Mj4kejhjixbBGvTmiO70bjQJ6ao41Wziaq_tUvuOmx

Elaine Roberto Navas

Opinion:"Drawing and art is a natural thing as eating and walking. Whenever I can draw or paint, I feel like my true self, If I don’t allow myself to work I feel uneasy and angry that I’m not using it.."
